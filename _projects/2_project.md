Converted an electric vehicle into an autonomous intra-campus shuttle featuring lane-following and obstacle detection capabilities to ensure safe and centered navigation.

- Designed and actuated control systems for braking, acceleration, and steering.
- Integrated a closed-loop feedback system using Pixhawk, RGB-D cameras, and LiDAR sensors.
- Enabled autonomous and remote operation for enhanced safety and reliability.
